What are sharks

Sharks are a large group of fish that have been around for almost 400 million years. Widley reconised as ruthless predators and a danger to humans that most often not the case. They are beautiful creatures perfectley adapted to thier enviroment and their vital role as apex predators of the oceans. There are many diffrent species large and small found in all areas of the globe from warm carribean waters of the gulf of mexico to the freezing waters of the arctic.
While they have been around for millions of years still so little is known about these creatures and what effect humans are having on the populations of all species of shark. To help save and protect these creatures it is important to understand that threats to them and what we can do to help them and reduce the dammage humans cause to the oceans.
